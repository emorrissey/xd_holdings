Founding Partners

Ed Morrissey

Ed is an industry expert in applying creative, user-centered design and development principles to help businesses extend their brands online and better connect with consumers, employees, partners and investors.

Ed grew his previous firm to be named one of AdWeek’s Top 20 Interactive Agencies and has developed award-winning web-based solutions for a variety of clients within the technology, consumer packaged goods (CPG), healthcare, government, oil and gas, media and financial services industries. A frequent industry speaker, Ed’s clients have included such brands as Hewlett Packard, Sony, Microsoft, Bausch & Lomb, Continental Airlines and Exxon Mobil.

John Simanowitz

A graduate of Murray State University, John is an authority in the practical application of web-based solutions. He solves complex brand, marketing and communication issues for a wide range of clients. With broad experiences in branding, marketing, system usability, online public relations and interactive social media, John aligns brand promise with product reality.

John has led award-winning developments for public and secure projects with the healthcare, education, consumer packaged goods (CPG), technology, entertainment and service industries.
