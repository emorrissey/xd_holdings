How we Invest

A critical aspect of any partnership today is flexibility. The world is changing so rapidly, every partner needs the ability to pivot and respond accordingly. Thus, we most often invest a mix of services for equity, capital or traditional fee for services - based upon the stage of the organization, current funding status and what is most beneficial to ensure success.


Services for Equity

As a leading UX consultancy, Integrity brings to the table a depth and breadth of expertise that is invaluable to start-ups and new ventures.

Exchanging our services for equity produces a win/win long-term relationship where both parties have true skin in the game.

Capital

Integrity will selectively invest in world-changing ideas that plan to disrupt the status quo.

We also invest follow on capital in 100% of the partnerships we exchange services for equity at the onset. 

Fee for Service

For those trying to conserve equity, or who just want to scale an innovation team, our traditional fee for service engagement model allows immediate team and productivity.

We look for long-term programs where we can partner with our clients to change the world.
